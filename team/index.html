Visiting students (for undergraduates and graduates)

Visiting students of all ranks should aim for scientific research with publishing high-quality papers as a primary purpose of the visit. Strong academic record is expected. Long-term stay (minimum 5 months) is expected. If interested, please send me your CV, transcript, and other supporting documents (if any) by individual PDF files (compressed package is not recommended).

Internship positions for undergraduate students

If you are an undergraduate student with ShanghaiTech University and interested in our research, please feel free to contact me (tangyt1(AT)shanghaitech.edu.cn).

Openings for Msc, Ph.D. students

Our group actively recurites 2-3 graduate students with background in program analysis, software testing, or Android apps (NLP or deep learning experiences is a plus) per year.
Currently, for Ph.D. programme, SIST only supports Msc-Ph.D. programme (硕博连读/硕转博计划).
Please drop me (tangyt1(AT)shanghaitech.edu.cn) an email with your CV and transcript if you have interest.

Openings for Research Assistants/Associates, Postdocs, Research Assistant Professors (title:助理研究员)

We are seeking Post-Doc Researchers with strong interests in software engineering, system security. A Ph.D. degree in software engineering, system security or related areas is required. After finishing Post-Doc research, top Post-Docs have the opportunity to join ShanghaiTech as research assistant professors（助理研究员). Please drop me (tangyt1(AT)shanghaitech.edu.cn) an email with your CV if you have interest. Please also reference the job post: here.
