About

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Department of Computing of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong.


I received the B.Sc. degree in computer science from Jilin Univeristy, P.R. China in 2013, where I worked with Prof. Lan Huang.


My research interests focus on software engineering, specifically, applying program analysis techniques in configurable application, system security (Android), deep learning, etc.
