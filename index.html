About

I am a software engineering Ph.D. Candidate at Department of Computing of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong. As a member of Software Development and Management Lab(now, I move to cyber security and privacy laboratory with Dr. Daniel Luo), I'm working under supervision of Dr.Hareton Leung and Dr. Daniel Xiapu Luo


I received the B.Sc. degree in computer science from Jilin Univeristy, P.R. China in 2013, where I worked with Prof. Lan Huang.


My research interests focus on software engineering, specifically, applying program analysis techniques in configurable application, system security (Android), deep learning, etc.
