    Openings for Msc, Ph.D. students

    Our group actively recurites 2-3 graduate students with background in program analysis, software testing, or Android apps (NLP or deep learning experiences is a plus) per year.
    Students with industrial experience are also welcome!
    Currently, for Ph.D. programme, SIST only supports Msc-Ph.D. programme (硕博连读/硕转博计划).
    Please drop me an email with your CV and transcript if you have interest.

    Openings for Research Assistants (RA)

    We are seeking Research Assistants with strong interests in software engineering, system security, deep learning. For the RA position, a MSc degree in software engineering, system security, deep learning or related areas is a must. Please drop me an email with your CV if you have interest.

    Openings for Postdocs, Research Assistant Professors

    We are seeking Postdoc Researchers with strong interests in software engineering, system security. A Ph.D. degree in software engineering, system security or related areas is required. After finishing Postdoc research, top Postdocs have the opportunity to join ShanghaiTech as research assistant professors（助理研究员). For research assistant professors（助理研究员) positions, candidates must have at least 2 years postdoc research. Please drop me an email with your CV if you have interest. Please also reference the job post: here.
