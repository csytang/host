Prole
Dr.Tang is a UK Lecturer (equiv. to Assistant Professor in US system) in Software Engineering
at the School of Computing Science at University of Glasgow (Scotland, UK) interested in
AI4SE, SE4AI, empirical software engineering, variability in software systems, as well as open
source sustainability and software engineering for traditional and AI-enabled systems. Developing
mechanisms, techniques, and tools to improve program comprehension in software systems,
often focused on systems. Also interested in open-source sustainability and software engineering
for AI-enabled systems.
